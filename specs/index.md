## Terminology

The key words "MUST", "MUST NOT", "REQUIRED", "SHALL", "SHALL 
NOT", "SHOULD", "SHOULD NOT", "RECOMMENDED",  "MAY", and
"OPTIONAL" in these documents are to be interpreted as described in
[RFC 2119](https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc2119).
